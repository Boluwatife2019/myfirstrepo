Welcome to Agromobile farm

AGROMOBILE farm is a registered farm in Nigeria with number RC2826545. It is a mobile farming platform that is into both agricultural investment and animal safe keeping. The investment option aims to make money for you with your invested capital over an agreed period at a stated minimum return on investment (ROI) on animals and crops. The animal keeping offers an avenue for you to raise whatever animal you want in our farm at a fixed monthly payment. The farm has been in operation for several years within Ibadan in Oyo state, Nigeria. The farm is the company, the animals and crops are the stock and their development, offspring and yield is your dividend.
